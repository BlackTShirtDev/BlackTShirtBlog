Advent of Code Day 1: Fear the Trebuchet

The coding has begun. And with it the end of (hopefully) one of my final classes. I'm not sure really how to feel about it though. On one hand it feels like a weight being lifted, I almost have time for personal projects and to just seem more present. But also I expected to feel more... smart? Something like this made me a better programmer, a better student. I don't really feel like that right now. I guess the man take away would be that the biggest trait you need for graduate school isn't brains but perserverance. Test
